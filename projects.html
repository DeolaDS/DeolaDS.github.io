Murdoch University Job Portal

For my final year IT project, my team and I made a job portal for Murdoch University, Dubai. The project was to improve and customise an existing job portal, and to make it exclusive to Murdoch University, Dubai students.The project took around 4 months.

I was responsible for the backend development of the website. I was also responsible for designing the mockups and protoypes, which were done with Adobe XD. The portal was developed with Django, HTML/CSS, and PostgreSQL.

Students can create their profile using their unique student ID, include their skills, search and apply for jobs. Employers can post jobs and find suitable candidates according to their required skill sets. It allows an admin to generate and download statistical data such as number of students had got a job through the portal, the number of users at a given time period as required by the KHDA.
